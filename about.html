About CloakNote

CloakNote was built on the principle that privacy is a fundamental human right. In an era of mass surveillance and data harvesting, we provide a simple, secure way to share information without leaving a digital footprint.

Our Security Model

Unlike traditional note-sharing services, CloakNote uses Client-Side Encryption. When you write a note, your browser generates a random key and encrypts the content using AES-GCM before any link is created. The encryption key never leaves your device. It is stored only in the URL fragment (the part after the #), which is never sent to any server.

Key Features

  • ✨ E2E Encryption: Browser-based AES-GCM.
  • 🔥 Zero Persistence: No database storage.
  • ⌛ Burn-on-Open: Local-enforced self-destruction.
  • 🚫 No Tracking: No accounts or identity required.

Who is this for?

Developers sharing API keys, HR professionals sending sensitive employee details, or anyone who needs to send a "one-time" password or secret safely.
