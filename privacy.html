Privacy Policy

CloakNote is designed with privacy as the core principle. We do not read, track, or store your notes.

What We Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for your name, email, or identity.
  • No Content Access: Your notes are encrypted locally. We never see their content.
  • Minimal Stats: We use Firebase for basic metrics (visit counts) to improve the tool.

Encrypted Notes

All notes are encrypted in your browser before a link is created. The encryption key is stored in the URL fragment (#), which stays in your browser and is never sent to our servers.

Data Retention

Since we don't use a database for note content, your notes exist only within the encrypted links you share. Once a link is "burned" (either by local storage marking or the link being lost), the data is effectively gone.

Contact

For privacy concerns, reach out via the official channels on the main page.
